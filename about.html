About

Welcome to the Geriatic Humane Society here in Nashville. Our founders noticed a vast increase in the number of abandoned elderly individuals of the baby boomer persuasion and decided to step into the fray to mitigate the inevitable rise in elderly homelessness. Please find it in your heart of hearts to adopt a grandparent to become part of your family.

Look at our satisfied adopted grandparents!
